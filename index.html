Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2025  |  Last Updated: March 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Finish Line Home Buyers ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you submit an inquiry, fill out a lead form, or otherwise interact with us — including through third-party platforms such as Facebook and Instagram.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our services and do not submit your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Property Information: Address, ownership status, and details about the property you wish to sell.
  • Financial Information: General information about your mortgage, liens, or financial situation as it relates to your property, provided voluntarily by you.
  • Communication Data: Records of your correspondence with us, including messages, calls, and form submissions.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and pages visited if you interact with any of our online properties.

3. How We Collect Your Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you when you complete a lead form, contact us by phone, email, or text message.
  • Through third-party advertising platforms (e.g., Facebook Lead Ads, Instagram) when you respond to our advertisements.
  • Through public property records and databases used in the normal course of real estate business.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Contact you regarding your property and our home-buying services.
  • Evaluate and make offers to purchase real property.
  • Communicate updates, follow-ups, or information relevant to your inquiry.
  • Improve our services and marketing efforts.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to unaffiliated third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in limited circumstances, including:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist us with communications, CRM management, or transaction coordination, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Business Partners: Real estate professionals involved in the closing or transaction process, as necessary.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets.

6. Text Messaging & Communications Consent

By submitting your phone number through any of our forms or advertisements, you consent to receive calls and/or text messages from Finish Line Home Buyers regarding your inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message or by contacting us directly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you wish to have your information deleted, please contact us using the information below.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Withdraw consent for certain uses of your data.
  • Request that we stop contacting you for marketing purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information provided in Section 10.
